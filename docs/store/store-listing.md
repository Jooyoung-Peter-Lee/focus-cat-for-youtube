## Detailed Description

Does YouTube eat your evenings? Focus Cat helps you watch intentionally.

Set a daily time limit, and when you've had enough, your cat gently blocks the screen until tomorrow. No harsh punishment — just a soft nudge from a mildly judgmental feline companion.

**Core Features (Free)**
• Set a custom daily watch-time limit (minutes)
• Warning banner at 90% of your limit
• Soft Mode: overlay appears but can be dismissed
• Removes distracting recommendations, Shorts, and sidebar clutter
• Daily reset at midnight

**Pro Features ($4.99 one-time)**
• Hard Mode: overlay cannot be dismissed — real commitment
• +5 min extension button: grant yourself a little extra when you truly need it

**Privacy First**
All data lives entirely on your device. No account required. No analytics. No tracking.
