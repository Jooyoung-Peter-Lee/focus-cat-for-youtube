How it works

No sign-up. No account. Three steps and you're in control.

1

Set your daily limit

Open the Focus Cat settings page and enter how many minutes of YouTube you want per day. The default is 60 minutes. Set it to 0 to disable the limit entirely.

2

Watch YouTube normally

Focus Cat tracks your watch time silently in the background using Chrome's extension APIs. Nothing is recorded or sent to any server. Your data stays on your device.

3

Focus Cat steps in

At 90% of your limit, a brief warning banner appears. When the limit is reached, an overlay covers the player. You choose how strict it is — Soft or Hard mode.

Everything you need to focus

A small, well-built tool. No subscription. No tracking. Just the features that matter.

⏱️

Daily time limit

Set a daily YouTube watching limit in minutes. Focus Cat uses timestamp delta tracking — your count stays accurate even when your browser or laptop restarts.

  • Any limit from 1 to 1,440 minutes (24 hours)
  • Set to 0 to disable the limit
  • Resets at midnight, your local time
  • 90% warning banner before the limit is reached
🐱

Blocking overlay

When your limit is reached, a friendly overlay with an animated cat appears directly over the YouTube video player. It shows how much you watched and your daily limit.

  • Covers the video player, not the whole page
  • Shows today's total watch time
  • Two modes: dismissible (Soft) or locked (Hard)
  • Encouraging message — not a punishment
🧹

Content filtering

Reduce temptation before you even reach your limit. Hide the parts of YouTube that pull you into watching when you didn't plan to.

  • Hide home page recommendation grid
  • Hide right-sidebar "Up next" list
  • Block Shorts from home and sidebar
  • Search and Subscriptions remain fully usable

Soft mode vs Hard mode

Choose the level of commitment that fits your goal.

Free

The overlay appears when your limit is reached, but you keep the final decision. Useful when you want awareness without strict enforcement.

  • ✓ No dismiss button — locked until midnight
  • ✓ Keyboard shortcuts blocked on the overlay
  • ✓ Requires an active Pro license
